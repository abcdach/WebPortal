Intelligent Automatization System

SDSU-G EE and CE Capstone Project [ Mentor: David Chkhaidze ]

Our project aims to create, integrative and easy to use smart house system, based on MAXIOT - the Internet of things. With other modern technologies we used some AI features, to make day to day activities even easier to manage.
